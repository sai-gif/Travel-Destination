Glacier National Park, located in Montana, USA, is famous for its breathtaking glaciers, pristine lakes, and rugged mountain terrain. It's a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, offering hiking trails, wildlife viewing, and the scenic Going-to-the-Sun Road.

Grand Canyon National Park

The Grand Canyon, located in Arizona, is one of the world's most iconic natural wonders. Carved by the Colorado River, it offers awe-inspiring vistas of colorful rock layers that have been shaped over millions of years. Visitors can enjoy hiking, rafting, and exploring the South Rim and North Rim viewpoints.
